Set directly across from a school and park, this is the kind of home that makes everyday life easier from the moment you move in.

The main floor is bright, open and designed for real living, with 10-foot ceilings, hardwood flooring and a natural flow between the kitchen, dining and living spaces anchored by a gas fireplace.

Downstairs is where this home separates itself — a fully finished lower level complete with a custom wet bar, media space and games area that transforms how you entertain, relax and spend time together.

Outside, a private backyard with stamped concrete patio and pergola creates a natural extension of the home for summer evenings and weekend gatherings.

With a rare double garage featuring 12-foot ceilings, a four-car driveway and key updates already completed, this is a home built for everyday function, long-term comfort and family-friendly living on Hamilton Mountain.
